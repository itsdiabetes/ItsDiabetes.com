Type 1 Diabetes: An Anonymous Journal

Diabetes may be an incurable disease, but at least it's not cancer. When properly managed, quality of life can be on par with non-diabetics. A 2015 study indicates that estimated life expectancy for Type 1 patients is only 11 years reduced for men and 13 years reduced for women. Things aren't great, but they could be far worst.

This is my anonymous diabetic journal; please keep my identity confidental. I'm sharing this information for those recently diagnosed or uncertain about their T1D.
