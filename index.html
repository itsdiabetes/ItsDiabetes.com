Diabetes: An Anonymous Journal

Costs

The unfortunate reality is managing diabetes is expensive. This fact of life is true across the entire globe, be that the United Kingdom or the United States. However, in the United States, the healthcare system contributes to a unique set of hardships unparalleled with the rest of the developed world. There is neither free nor universal healthcare in the United States. This means that there is a physical, emotional, and financial cost for managing in the United States. Unfortunately, the financial cost is unbearable.
